10. On the issue of venue, plaintiff has the burden to offer prima facie proof, and defendant is not permitted to offer rebuttal evidence. See Surgitek, Bristol-Myers Corp. v. Abel, 997 S.W.2d 598, 602 (Tex. 1999).

11. On the issue of joinder, plaintiff has the initial burden to offer prima facie proof of the four joinder elements listed in Section 15.003(a). Surgitek, 997 S.W.2d at 602-03.
